Mission

Our mission is to reduce the underlying systemic discrimination that perpetuates economic and racial inequality.

We do this by making it easy for celebrities, public figures, and influencers to get involved in social movements, connecting promoters with meaningful products, creating a network of motivated individuals, and teaching others how to take action using a bottom-up approach.
